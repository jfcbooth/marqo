How It Works

1Discover

Find the hidden gems in your local communities by searching our database for partnered locations. Search by city, name, or zipcode to find the great local offerings. Sweet!

2Support

Spend as little as $10 for the chance to win something extraordinary and join the Marqo community. Once you're in one of our partnered locations, simply find our poster and scan the QR code to check you in. Finally, snap a pic of your reciept and send it in to us through the app. Together, we can build stonger communities across the world.

3Win

Your support to these small businesses make a difference for your amazing community, and that's an awesome thing to do (win!). One person is picked at random to win the prize specified (win!). The local business gets support from a brand new network of people, allowing them to continue their awsome craft (the biggest win of all!).
